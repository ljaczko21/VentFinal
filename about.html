General Information

About Us

Vent is a website that is accessible and available to anyone. This is a safe space to use where anyone can share their feelings and get help from our trained volunteers 24/7. Volunteers will connect emotionally and personally with people to make them feel heard and allow them to alleviate their anxiety. Our volunteers are screened and trained to handle a wide range of conversations, from trauma to identity to mental health and many more topics. Our site also provides meditation and mindfulness tips to reduce anxiety and stress.

Our Mission

Vent aims to create an approachable, accessible and affordable atmosphere for those who are having a hard time coping with stress and/or anxiety. We hope to foster a safe environment so that those who have been harassed and/or abused can reach out and receive the help that they deserve. Our overall mission is to ensure that people get the help they need, in the way that they want to receive it no matter what the cause of seeking assistance is.

Contact Information

We can be reached at contact.us.at.vent@gmail.com 24 hours a day 7 days a week.

Company Bio

Our overall mission is to provide a safe space for people to de-stress and gain relief through interpersonal conversation and relaxing home therapies, no matter what their circumstances are.
